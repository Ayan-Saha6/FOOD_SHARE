About FoodShare

At FoodShare, we are committed to reducing food waste and fighting hunger by connecting donors and NGOs. Our platform streamlines the donation process, ensuring surplus food reaches those in need quickly and safely.

Since our inception, we have collaborated with numerous restaurants and non-profit organizations to impact thousands of lives. By fostering a network of care and support, we aim to build a future where no one goes to bed hungry.

We currently operate in multiple states across India, with a vision to expand nationwide, empowering communities and bringing hope to those who need it most.
